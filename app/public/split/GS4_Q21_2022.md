### **Impact of War on Different Aspects of Society
1. Human Tragedy- Russian casualties approaching **1.4 million in 2025
1. Displacement Crisis- Over **14 million Ukrainians
1. Global Food Insecurity
1. Energy Volatility- "energy poverty" for low-income households.
1. Environmental "Ecocide"- Eg- destruction of the Kakhovka Dam
1. "War Trauma" for an entire generation of children
1. Erosion of "Rules-Based Order" (violation of the UN Charter)

1. Adherence to principles of **Just War Theory - **Jus ad Bellum (the right to go to war) and **Jus in Bello (right conduct within war).

1. **a. Mass Casualties
