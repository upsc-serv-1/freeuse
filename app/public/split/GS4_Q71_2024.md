1. Constitutional Morality- Prioritizing the spirit of the Constitution over individual interests.
1. Decisions must be tested against the **"Gandhian Talisman"- how does it help the weakest?
1. Selflessness- Acting solely in public interest, without seeking any benefits for oneself.
1. Probity and Integrity- Eg- Singapore Civil Service zero-tolerance ethics policy.
1. Objectivity- Making choices on merit alone.
1. Confidentiality with Responsibility. Eg- EU data ethics norms.
