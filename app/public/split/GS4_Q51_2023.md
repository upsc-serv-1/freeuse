## Question 51 (Year: 2023 | Paper: GS IV)

**Question ID: 2023-gs4-q51-civilsdaily**

**2023 - In the context of the work environment, differentiate between ‘coercion’ and ‘undue influence’ with suitable examples.** [Year: 2023] [Marks: 10]

### Answer

**Work culture **refers to the shared values, norms, behaviour and ethical climate that shape how individuals interact, make decisions and exercise authority within an organisation.

“The culture of any organisation is shaped by the worst behaviour the leader is willing to tolerate.”

| **Coercion** | **Undue Influence** |

| --- | --- |

| use of **force, threat, intimidation or punishment **to compel a person to act against their will. | use of **position, authority, relationship, persuasion or subtle pressure **to unfairly influence a person’s decision |

| Forceful compulsion through abuse or threat | Subtle manipulation through emotional means |

| Consent of employee is absent | Involves consent but is distorted |

| Open and explicit | Indirect and concealed |

| Clearly illegal. Eg- violation of POSH Act | Often unethical, legally grey |

| Intensity is high and immediate | Intensity is gradual and psychological |

| Mostly used through authority or hierarchy | Mostly leveraged by personal and emotional connections |

| Results in act under duress or whistleblowing or resignation | May face crisis of conscience or guilt |

| Remedy - Institutional and legal measures. Eg-ICC complaint | Remedy - Code of ethics and code of conduct |

| Violation of autonomy | Violation of fairness |

| Threat of transfer for not clearing illegal file | Hint of promotion for approving biased decision |

### Way Forward for a Healthy and Ethical Work Culture

1.** Institutionalizing 360-Degree Feedback**

2.** Strengthening Whistleblower Protection Mechanisms**

3.** Civil services board for transparent transfer and posting**

4.** Adopting Nolan Principles **of **openness**

5.** Promoting "Psychological Safety"**

6.** Regular Ethics Audits**

7.** Democratizing the Workplace**

**"Highest Perfection" **in duty can never be achieved through fear or manipulation. For effective work culture, authority must be exercised through **competence and trust**
