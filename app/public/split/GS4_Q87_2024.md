## Question 87 (Year: 2024 | Paper: GS IV)

**2024 - “Faith is of no avail in the absence of strength. Faith and strength, both are essential to accomplish any great work.” - Sardar Patel [Year: 2024] [Marks: 10]

### Answer

This quote reflects his belief that while **Faith (vision, conviction, or idealism) provides the direction, **Strength (willpower, resources, and courage) provides the momentum. Faith without strength becomes helpless idealism and strength without faith becomes blind force.
### **Values Involved
1. ** Pragmatic Idealism
1. ** Fortitude
1. ** Conviction
1. ** Resilience
### **Faith is of no avail in the absence of strength
1. Idealism without action is ineffective - Belief alone cannot change reality.
1. Without **strength of mind, faith can become weak. Eg- rising suicide among students
1. Vision without resources remains symbolic. Eg- Climate pledges without funding
1. Laws without enforcement are hollow. Eg- persistence of child labour
1. Policy without capacity fails delivery. Eg- food wastage in FCI godowns
1. Diplomacy without deterrence invites aggression. Eg- failure of **hindi-Chini bhai bhai
1. Hope without discipline fades. Eg- success in UPSC requires consistency in hard work.
**Need for faith and strength for great work
1. Faith gives vision, strength gives execution. Eg- **Green Revolution- Vision of food security + scientific and administrative strength.
1. Faith gives **strength to bounce back after failures. Eg- ISRO successfully launching Chandrayan 3 after failure of Chandrayan 2
1. Faith inspires people while strength organises them. Eg- **Freedom movement- Gandhian ideals + organisational networks of INC.
1. Faith gives moral authority while strength build institutions. Eg- India’s faith in strategic autonomy led to creation of NAM.
1. Faith guides ends, strength manages means. Eg- **Climate action- Sustainability ideals + renewable technology.
1. Faith resolves why, strength resolves how. Eg- **Women empowerment- Equality principle + legal reservations.
1. In a crisis, Faith provides the **mental resilience, while Strength provides the resourcefulness to solve the problems
1. Social reform requires the **faith that humans can change, backed by the Strength to enforce that change. Eg- Raja Rammohan Roy’s advocacy for Sati Abolition Act.
Patel unified 562 states by combining his **faith in a single India with the **strength of his decisive, iron will.
