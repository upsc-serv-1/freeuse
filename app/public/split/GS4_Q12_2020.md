## Question 12 (Year: 2020 | Paper: GS IV)

**Question ID: 2020-gs4-q12-civilsdaily**

**2020 - “Education is not an injunction, it is an effective and pervasive tool for all round development of an individual and social transformation”. Examine the New Education Policy, 2020 (NEP, 2020) in light of the above statement.** [Year: 2020] [Marks: 10]

### Answer

"Education is the manifestation of perfection already in man." - **Swami Vivekananda**

Education goes beyond instruction. It is a **transformative force** shaping character, capability, and societal progress.

### Education as a tool for all-round development of an individual

1. **Cognitive Agility-** Develops critical thinking and problem-solving skills

2. **Emotional Resilience-** Fosters self-awareness and empathy

3. **Ethical Moorings-** Instills values like integrity and compassion

4. **Physical and Aesthetic Growth** through sports

5. **Vocational Dignity-** fosters a "dignity of labor" and life-skills.

6. **Social Intelligence-** Schools act as "mini-societies"

### Education as a tool for social transformation

1. **Social mobility-** acts as the "great leveler"

2. **Promotion of Secularism and Tolerance**

3. **Promotes democratic participation and ‘informed citizenry’**

4. **Gender Equality-** Eg- "Beti Bachao Beti Padhao" movement

5. **Develops scientific temper** and **critical thinking**

6. Acts as a **change catalyst.** Eg- rising **‘working women’**

### NEP 2020- Empowering Individual and Society

1. **Holistic Individual Growth** through **5+3+3+4 Structure** and Activity-based learning in Anganwadis

2. **Builds character through value Education-** integrates "Constitutional Values" (like Seva, Ahimsa, and Environmental conservation) into the curriculum.

3. **Promotes Multidisciplinary Learning-** Eg- Arts-science integration.

4. **Flexibility and Multiple Entry/Exit Options** through "Academic Bank of Credits" (ABC) ensures that individuals aren't punished for life interruptions.

5. **Vocational Integration** from Class 6 promotes **dignity of labor**

6. **Linguistic Empowerment** through 3 language formula bridges the gap between home and school

7. **Equitable Social Transformation -** Eg- **Gender Inclusion Fund** increases retention of girl students.

8. Ensuring **accessibility through Special Education Zones (SEZs)** in "Aspirational Districts"

9. **Technological Democratization-** The National Educational Technology Forum (NETF) aims to use AI and digital tools to reach the "last mile."

10. **Teacher Empowerment-** Eg- **NISHTHA** program to train teachers in innovative, learner-centric methodologies.

### Challenges in Implementation

1. **Low funding-** stagnant at **4.6% of GDP** (target of **6% of GDP**)

2. **Digital Divide-** Eg- **digital apartheid** in rural India

3. **Logistics of Multilingualism-** Challenge in creating textbooks in **22+ regional languages**

4. **Federal issue -** Eg- TN opposition to **3-language formula**

5. Challenges in **mindset shift from rote to competency.**

Effective implementation of NEP can turn India into a **Global Knowledge Superpower** (Vishwaguru)
