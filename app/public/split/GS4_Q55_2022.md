## Question 55 (Year: 2022 | Paper: GS IV)

**Question ID: 2022-gs4-q55-civilsdaily**

**2022 - Wisdom lies in knowing what to reckon with and what to overlook. An officer being engrossed with the periphery, ignoring the core issues before him, is no rare in the bureaucracy. Do you agree that such preoccupation of an administrator leads to travesty of justice to the cause of effective service delivery and good governance? Critically evaluate.** [Year: 2022] [Marks: 10]

### Answer

When a civil servant becomes a slave to periphery or "form," administration becomes an end in itself, at the cost of the citizen it was meant to serve.

**Core Vs Periphery**

| **The Core (Substance)** | **The Periphery (Form)** |

| --- | --- |

| Ensuring **Justice **and **Dignity. | Excessive **Documentation **and **Rules. |

| **Solving the Problem **at hand. | Following the **Standard Operating Procedure. |

| **Spirit of the Law. | **Letter of the Law. |

| **Outcome-Oriented **Governance. | **Process-Oriented **Bureaucracy. |

**Preoccupation with periphery - travesty of justice**

1.** Red-tapism over responsiveness- **Eg- **Munger-Ganga Bridge **in Bihar was delayed by nearly 14 years due to inter-departmental disputes

2. It is a **"corruption of omission"**- may be legally clean, but ethically bankrupt.

3. Periphery breeds a **"mechanical" mindset **where officials **lose empathy. **Eg- denial of emergency care to critically ill patient without documentation

4.** Process compliance over public welfare. Eg- Eg- PDS exclusion of poor due to biometric authentication failures (Jharkhand case)

5.** Encourages moral abdication - **Officials transfer moral responsibility to the rulebook (banality of evil). Eg- encroachment demolitions without rehabilitation plan.

6. Facilitates **coercive corruption. Eg- ‘speed money’ for faster file clearance

7. It leads to **Process becoming Punishment. Eg- **complexity in environmental clearances

8. "By-the-book" culture **stifles innovation and creativity.**

9.** Resource Wastage- **Eg- cost and time overruns in PPP projects due to delays in land procurement

10.** Policy Paralysis- **Fear of audit or the "3C" (CBI, CVC, CAG) leads to "Defensive Bureaucracy."

### Way Forward

1.** Adopting the "Karmayogi" Mindset- **"solution-providers" rather than "file-pushers".

**Vision of civil servants**

**1. Imaginative & Innovative**

**2. Proactive & Polite**

**3. Professional & Progressive**

**4. Energetic & Enabling**

**5. Transparent & Tech-enabled**

**6. Creative & Constructive**

2.** Institutionalizing Social Audits- **Eg- Meghalaya social audit law

3.** Protection for Honest Discretion **by amending Section 17A of the Prevention of Corruption Act.

4.** Promoting "Co-Production"- **Involving NGOs and SHGs to deliver services. Eg- **Kudumbashree**

5.** Application of Gandhi’s Talisman **in decision making

The ultimate goal of an administrator is to ensure that the **"True Intent" **of the Constitution - Justice, Liberty, and Equality - is delivered.
