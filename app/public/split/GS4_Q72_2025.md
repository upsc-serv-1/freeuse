## Question 72 (Year: 2025 | Paper: GS IV)

**Question ID: 2025-gs4-q72-civilsdaily**

**2025 - It is said that for an ethical work culture, there must be code of ethics in place in every organisation. To ensure value-based and compliance-based work culture, what suitable measures would you adopt in your work place?** [Year: 2025] [Marks: 10]

### Answer

An ethical work culture is the **collective mindset and behavior **of an organization that **prioritizes moral principles **over mere profit or procedure.

### Importance of a Code of Ethics

1.** Uniformity and consistency in decision-making **based on values

2.** Building Public Trust- **Eg- Tata Group

3.** Ensures Legal and Regulatory Compliance **in spirit rather than letter

4.** Employee Pride and Retention- **Eg- Patagonia’s employee turnover rate due to commitment to the environment.

5.** Prevention of Corruption- **Eg- PepsiCo's code provides specific dollar limits for business gifts

6.** Institutionalizing Accountability **based on principles like **trusteeship **and **fairness**

7.** Safeguarding Reputation- **Acts as a shield during crises

8.** Promoting Inclusivity- **Eg- **HanesBrands’s **"Excellence through Diversity" principle.

9.** Stakeholder Protection through transparency. **Eg- H&M publishes 98.5% of its supplier addresses

10.** Conflict of Interest Control. Eg- US Federal Ethics disclosure system.

### Measures

### Value-Based Measures

1.** Ethical Leadership- **Leaders must "walk the talk." Eg- **Narayana Murthy **following simple living to set a culture of austerity and integrity.

2.** Participative Value Formulation- **Involving employees in drafting the code **to create ownership.**

3.** Ethical Training- **Eg- **Kellogg Company **uses interactive quizzes and " grain farm" themed storytelling to teach ethics.

4.** Integration into Appraisals- **Rewarding "how" a goal was met, not just "what" was achieved. Egadding an "Empathy Score" to their performance reviews.

5.** Psychological Safety- **Eg- **Microsoft’s "Growth Mindset" culture **encourages learning from failures rather than hiding them.

### Compliance-Based Measures

6.** Robust Whistleblower Mechanism- **Secure and anonymous channels for reporting.

7.** Appointment of a Chief Ethics Officer **to oversee integrity initiatives.

8.** Regular Ethical Audits **for monitoring of financial and behavioral conduct. Eg- **"Social Audits" **by Big Four accounting firms.

9.** Clear Disciplinary Protocols- **Eg- The **"Zero Tolerance" policy **for sexual harassment in **Goldman Sachs.**

10.** Leveraging RegTech- **Using technology to flag irregularities. Eg- AI-driven software to detect potential insider trading in real-time.

11.** Transparency in process. **Eg- **GeM portal in public procurement.

Building ethical work culture requires a **Symphonic Approach **based on **moral clarity **through CoE, and **robust systems **for compliance.
